AI-curated security research feed, auto-updated daily from 300+ security blogs, researchers, and vendors.

New articles are fetched, classified by an AI model, and published here with priority labels and tags. Critical findings are pinned to the top.

## How it works

1. **Fetch** -- RSS/Atom feeds are polled daily for new articles
2. **Extract** -- Article content is scraped and cleaned
3. **Classify** -- An AI model assigns category, priority, and tags
4. **Publish** -- Research articles are written as posts and deployed here

## Fork it

This project is open source. Fork the [repository](https://github.com/zAbuQasem/security-feed-monitor) and customize the feed list to build your own curated security feed.
