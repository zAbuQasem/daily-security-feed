SentinelOne reports on Russian actor UAT-11795 trojanizing popular remote tools (WebEx, Zoom, MobaXterm) to distribute Starland RAT through HTA→NSIS→Python loader chains with registry persistence and C2 via Polygon smart contract fallbacks and Telegram bots. Separately, ~300 imposter GitHub repositories masquerading as security tools distribute BoryptGrab infostealer via WinGUP DLL sideloading attacks, targeting passwords, payment details, and cryptocurrency wallets across 19 browsers and 32 wallet brands. U.S. and EU law enforcement actions also dismantled Russian bulletproof hosting services (Media Land, ML Cloud) supporting Lockbit, Play, and Blacksuit ransomware operations, with a $10M reward for intelligence on foreign government links.
