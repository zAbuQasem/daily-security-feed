Law enforcement arrested an Iranian cybercriminal responsible for $3.4B in damages against 150+ U.S. universities and extradited Peter Stokes (19, UNC3944/Scattered Spider) for social engineering attacks bypassing MFA during corporate breaches. Russian Intelligence (UNC5792/UNC4221) is conducting large-scale phishing targeting Signal users' backup recovery keys to decrypt message archives; a critical vulnerability is that re-registering with the same phone number doesn't invalidate compromised keys—users must manually generate a new recovery key. An unknown attacker breached the DHS Homeland Security Information Network (HSIN), an unclassified interagency intelligence platform, with authorities concerned about potential exposure of World Cup security planning and coordination protocols.
