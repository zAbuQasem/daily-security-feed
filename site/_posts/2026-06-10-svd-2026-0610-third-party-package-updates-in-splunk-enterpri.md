Splunk released advisories addressing multiple critical and high-severity CVEs in bundled third-party packages across Enterprise versions 9.3.13–10.4.0. Critical updates include Go compiler (go1.26.1) fixing six CVEs in the `compsup` binary, aiohttp (3.13.5) remediating CVE-2026-34516 and CVE-2026-34520 in Splunk Secure Gateway, MongoDB (7.0.31/8.0.20) addressing CVE-2026-4147/4148/4358, and PostgreSQL (17.8) fixing four CVEs. Upgrade paths are version-specific across the support matrix (9.3.13, 9.4.12, 10.0.7, 10.2.4, 10.4.0), with detailed remediation locations provided for each binary affected.
