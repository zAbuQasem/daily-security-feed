Red Canary describes how threat hunting programs scale from ad-hoc analyst investigations to structured operations. Challenges emerge when organizations expand across EDR, cloud, and identity data sources: inconsistent hunt outcomes, redundant query logic, and overwhelming data volumes. Solutions require normalizing raw data into analyst-ready schemas, centralizing reusable queries, and enabling laptop-scale analytics with tools like DuckDB and Parquet files. This approach maintains consistency and efficiency as threat hunting coverage expands across cloud, identity, and endpoint environments. The methodology directly supports detection engineering at scale and is practical for security operations centers managing diverse data sources.
