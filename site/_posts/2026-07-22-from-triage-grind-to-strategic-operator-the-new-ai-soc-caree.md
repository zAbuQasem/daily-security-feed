A SentinelOne thought leadership piece on how AI is reshaping SOC operations and analyst career paths. The article addresses a real operational challenge: SOC teams lack capacity to investigate >50% of daily alerts while attack chains execute in seconds (privilege escalation in ~30ms, persistence in <50s). AI automation is absorbing triage and correlation work, freeing analysts from high-volume queue management to focus on verdict review, strategic threat hunting, and workflow governance. The traditional tiered structure (L1/L2/L3) is being redefined by depth of expertise rather than processing volume, and analysts must develop new skills in AI validation, policy design, and risk translation.
