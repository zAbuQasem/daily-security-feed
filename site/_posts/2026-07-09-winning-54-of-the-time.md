Cisco Talos' weekly threat digest reports that China-nexus APT group UAT-7810 is actively expanding Operational Relay Box (ORB) networks by exploiting n-day vulnerabilities in unpatched Ruckus and ASUS routers to deploy upgraded LONGLEASH and DOGLEASH backdoors. These ORB networks serve as decentralized proxy infrastructure for secondary threat actors, allowing them to mask origins and bypass perimeter defenses. The newsletter also surfaces six AirDrop/Quick Share flaws, a hidden Tenda router authentication backdoor, Microsoft 365 phishing-as-a-service platform ARToken, and broader threat trends. Defenders should prioritize patching edge devices and monitor for suspicious proxying behavior and unauthorized connections on routers.
