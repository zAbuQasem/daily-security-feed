Datadog releases new tabletop incident response scenarios for the Backdoors & Breaches game, grounded in current threat landscape trends. Scenario 1 models backdoored third-party library integration leading to credential store compromise and resource exfiltration. Scenario 2 depicts cloud credential exposure in client-side code enabling IAM policy abuse and living-off-the-cloud exfil. Scenario 3 combines AI web application prompt injection vulnerability with Kubernetes service account privilege escalation and SaaS-tunneled command & control. Scenario 4 simulates undetected build pipeline tampering with credential exposure and persistent unauthorized outbound traffic—scenarios serve as practical defensive methodology for incident response training and threat modeling.
