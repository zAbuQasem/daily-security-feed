Cisco Talos describes a methodology for augmenting traditional reverse engineering tools with local AI agents. By exposing tool data through inter-process communication (IPC) protocols like COM, analysts can issue natural language prompts to automate tedious tasks such as function decompilation and call graph generation without manual vendor feature requests. This approach keeps sensitive binaries on the analyst's workstation, eliminating exfiltration risk, while demonstrating a broader pattern: any analysis tool managing structured data can become highly interactive through external scripting interfaces.
