Permiso launches the Risk Score Engine, a continuous identity risk scoring system that evaluates every identity (human, non-human, AI) across three dimensions: Behavior (anomaly detection against baseline), Likelihood (threat intelligence and compromise indicators), and Impact (access scope and blast radius). The system combines static posture with real-time behavioral signals from over 1,500 detection patterns, built on Permiso's Universal Identity Graph that unifies human users, service accounts, API keys, OAuth tokens, IAM roles, and AI agents into a single threat model. Session risk scores track active credential anomalies independent of historical risk, while score velocity measures rate of change to identify acute threats, and organization-wide scores aggregate risk across the entire environment benchmarked against peer enterprises.
