A practical guide to KAPE (Kroll Artifact Parser and Extractor), a free forensic tool for Windows incident response. The article covers extracting artifacts (Prefetch, Event Logs, Master File Table, USN Journal) from live systems or mounted disk images into a VHDX container, then parsing them into CSV or JSON formats for analysis. KAPE automates the collection and parsing of key Windows artifacts that reveal adversary activity, reducing manual effort during triage and investigation. The cheatsheet provides step-by-step GUI instructions for efficient multi-artifact collection and parsing workflows.
