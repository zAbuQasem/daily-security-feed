Trail of Bits' Patch the Planet initiative pairs expert engineers with GPT-5.5-Cyber to systematically discover and fix bugs across 19 critical open-source projects (Python, Go, PyCA, Sigstore, aiohttp, PyPI, etc.). In the first week, the effort yielded 64 pull requests and 51 issues, with 37 patches merged, including fixes for cryptographic interoperability bugs (AES-GCM in PyCA), resource/auth issues in aiohttp, X.509 parsing inconsistencies, and supply-chain hardening (release pipelines, CI auditing with zizmor, SBOM sidecars). The initiative demonstrates scaled vulnerability discovery and collaborative patching across critical infrastructure, with human expertise handling triage and ensuring high-quality fixes rather than noise.
