Red Canary's May 2026 threat intelligence report tracking the 10 most prevalent threats observed across customer environments. ClearFake continues to dominate via JavaScript-injected drive-by downloads paired with malicious copy-and-paste (paste-and-run) techniques; the report catalogs specific command examples used by HijackLoader, NetSupport Manager, and Atomic Stealer variants. Kali365, a new PhaaS platform, ranks #2 by automating OAuth device code phishing and adversary-in-the-middle attacks against Microsoft 365 accounts. Team PCP executed a notable supply chain compromise via a malicious TanStack CI workflow commit that propagated a self-propagating worm across npm and PyPI. Provides concrete indicators, command telemetry, and month-over-month threat prevalence tracking from a major SOC operator.
